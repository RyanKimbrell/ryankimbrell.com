ABOUT RYAN

Ryan Kimbrell is a Music Producer, Multi-Instrumentalist, Web Developer/Software Engineer, and 3D Computer Graphics Artist based in Los Angeles, California.

He earned a Master Degree (M.M.) in Contemporary Performance and Music Production from the Berklee College of Music in 2017 and was then selected for a Post-Graduate fellowship at Berklee from 2017-2018 where he further developed his production and sound design ability. He earned a Bachelor's Degree (B.M.) in Jazz Studies (Saxophone Performance) in 2016 from the University of Wisconsin-Madison.

Ryan's unique set of musical accomplishments make him the perfect addition to any team, playing saxophones and keyboards, singing, producing, and recording music at an expert level. He also specializes in synthesizers and the talkbox.

Learning computer programming has been one of Ryan's greatest accomplishments post-schooling, and he is proficient in Python (and Django Framework), JavaScript, Java, and C++. He completely programmed and launched this website and also spotifytools.net all by himself. Currently he is working on developing a suite of custom Audio Plugins for Music Production and Audio Editing in C++.
